About Nord-Line

Nord-Line LLC was founded in 2007 and at present Nord-Line is the official distributor of such companies as Nor-Maali (Finnish industrial coatings); Jotun (Norwegian concern Jotun Paints LLC); Defender (Laboratory Eurostyle LLC); AkzoNobel (Dutch multinational company - one of the leaders in the field of coatings). Many years of experience allowed to display the most high-quality manufacturers, at the same time covering all price ranges of products.

 

More than 10 years, Nord-Line offers supplies of anti-corrosion and flame retardant materials to the most significant projects in Russia and the near abroad. On each project, there is a compulsory full-fledged technical support of the works. The company's specialists track and analyze the state of materials throughout the life cycle of coatings. All the facilities are in immaculate condition.

MATERIALS

Materials

Fire protection compounds
Defender
Material Defender - a unique Russian product, has a positive proposal. It is used for fire protection of metal structures, closing all types of thin-layer compounds on water, organic and epoxy basis with the ability to provide fire resistance of 45, 60, 90, 120 and 150 minutes in various fire scenarios including the combustion of petroleum products. With similar characteristics, it is much cheaper than its foreign counterparts.
Anticorrosive coatings
Jotun
Anticorrosive materials of the Norwegian company JOTUN with localization of production in Russia. Provide an opportunity to solve the most complex tasks to protect against corrosion of oil and gas facilities and infrastructure operated in different climatic conditions, including extreme conditions of the Polar Circle. Produces high-tech primers, successfully used in factories of the manufacturer of metal structures, where the cycle is urgently needed without discontinuous production.
Fire protection compounds
AkzoNobel
Fireproof coatings of Chartek series provide cloudiness of fire resistance of 45, 60, 90, 120 minutes including hydrocarbon combustion. It has high processability and the widest geography of use in a wide variety of climatic conditions, including extreme marine and the conditions of the Polar Circle. Successfully applied in the factories of the manufacturer of metal structures, where it is necessary to ensure a continuous production cycle. Certified by the Lioyd (LR) and Det Norske Veritas (DNV) register, which allows the use of these materials on Russian and foreign projects.
